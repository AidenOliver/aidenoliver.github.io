Aiden Oliver

Web Developer & Open Source Contributor

Background

Born in London, raised in California. Developed a knack for building and design since I was 6 years old, which eventually landed my interest in web development. Now at the age of 20, I have committed most of my free time to web development, learning new frameworks, libraries, languages and design. Curiosity has always been my strong suit and I believe that is what propels my understanding of development, design and technology.

When creating a website or web app I keep in mind how responsive it is and test the site in a wide range of devices. As I develop and maintain a project I ensure that it is scalable and easy to understand so you can enhance a website as your company grows. First impressions are important, that's why I believe the user experience is the most important part to get right.

Skills

As of right now I specialize in Front-end development but I am committed to learning all the layers of Full-stack development and more. I have working knowledge of Windows PowerShell and Linux Bash command lines. I'm also certified in IT Support Specialist Fundamentals by Google. It's a five course specialization available on Coursera that teaches you about Windows, Linux, how to use each OS effectively and just computers in general.

I'm also just as good working independently on smaller projects too.
